Volcanoes in South America

Tallest

Ojo del Selado is the worlds tallest volcano and is located in South America.

Ojo del Selado has a height of 22,615'.

More info at Ojo del Selado

Most Dangerous

In 1985 Nevado del Ruiz erupted and melted the snow and ice at the summit.

This caused mudflows the swept many miles down river valleys and killed at least 23,000 people.

More info at Nevado del Ruiz

Most Active

Tungurahua is an active stratovolcano located in South America.

The volcanoes activity started in 1999 and is ongoing.
